About

Hello! I'm Sam. I hiked the Pacific Crest Trail in 2021.

I tracked my route on a Garmin watch and plotted the route here using the Mapbox API. Each color change is a different day of hiking. You can zoom in to see the exact route of each day. Hover over any of them to see info.

Or check out the animated route viz (click ANIMATION up top). Still working on all of this. HMU with ideas.
